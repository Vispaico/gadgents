# AI in ERP Systems

AI in ERP systems refers to using artificial intelligence inside enterprise resource planning platforms to automate routine work, support faster decisions, and help teams operate more intelligently. In this wiki, it connects to AI adoption polling and AI-enabled business decisions.

## Core idea

ERP platforms manage core business processes such as finance, purchasing, inventory, sales, and reporting. Adding AI capabilities can make these systems more proactive by turning data into recommendations, forecasts, automated summaries, and conversational assistance. The main value is practical: teams can spend less time on repetitive work and more time on analysis, planning, and action.

## Why this matters for ERP systems

AI in ERP systems can create impact across several business workflows:

- **Automation:** Reduces manual effort in repetitive operational tasks.
- **Decision support:** Helps users interpret data and act faster.
- **Forecasting:** Supports planning with predicted demand, inventory, or financial trends.
- **Recommendations:** Suggests next actions, such as purchase decisions or reporting priorities.
- **Productivity:** Provides assistant-like support for users working inside the ERP environment.
