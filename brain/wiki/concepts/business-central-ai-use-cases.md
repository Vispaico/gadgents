## Business Central AI Use Cases

Business Central AI Use Cases covers practical ways AI can improve Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central workflows, especially decision speed, automation, and operational productivity.

## Core idea

AI use cases in Business Central translate general AI benefits into ERP actions: helping users interpret data, automate repetitive work, recommend next steps, and forecast business conditions.

## Source connection

The source document [[summaries/2026-07-19-linkedin-post-by-clovex-solutions-private-limited-2d]] records a LinkedIn post by Clovex Solutions Private Limited asking which AI capability in Business Central would create the biggest impact. The post frames AI as reshaping business operations by helping teams work smarter, make faster decisions, and automate repetitive tasks.

## Main use cases highlighted

- **AI Copilot:** Assists users with natural-language guidance, task support, and faster access to Business Central insights.
- **Automated Reporting:** Reduces manual report preparation and helps teams monitor performance more consistently.
- **Purchase Recommendations:** Supports procurement decisions by surfacing suggested purchasing actions or replenishment guidance.
- **Predictive Forecasting:** Uses historical and current data to anticipate demand, inventory, financial, or sales trends; related to [[concepts/predictive-forecasting-in-business-applications]].

## Broader concept links

These use cases fit within [[concepts/ai-in-erp]], where AI is applied to enterprise systems to improve operational decisions, workflow automation, and strategic planning.

## Practical significance

The main value is prioritization: organizations adopting AI in Business Central need to choose which capability delivers the strongest business impact. The LinkedIn poll shows four candidate areas: AI Copilot, automated reporting, purchase recommendations, and predictive forecasting. Early engagement was modest, with 4 votes and 2 reactions, but the topic signals a broader shift toward AI-enabled ERP operations.

## Synthesis

Business Central AI use cases are most useful when tied to concrete business outcomes. AI Copilot and automated reporting tend to improve daily productivity, while purchase recommendations and predictive forecasting tend to improve planning and decision quality. Together, they show how AI can move Business Central from a transactional ERP system toward a more intelligent operating platform.
