# Curation Scope

## Overview

[[concepts/curation-scope]] explains how a source artifact is selected, preserved, and bounded for later use in the knowledge base. It is especially relevant when a document is intentionally narrow, isolated, or intended to stand alone rather than serve as the basis for a broader synthesis.

- A narrow scope can prevent overgeneralization from limited source material.
