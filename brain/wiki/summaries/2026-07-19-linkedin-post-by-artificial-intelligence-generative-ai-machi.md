# Summary

This LinkedIn post by **Artificial Intelligence, Generative AI, Machine Learning, Co** asks readers which department in their organization is most ready to create measurable business impact with AI today.

## Key Concepts

- AI is framed as an immediate **business priority**, not a future initiative.
- The post connects AI adoption to **enterprise AI**, **generative AI**, and **digital transformation**.
- It invites community debate about departmental readiness and measurable impact.
- Poll options include **Operations**, **HR**, **Finance**, and **Customer Support**.
- The post records only limited poll data: 3 votes and 4 days remaining; results are not shown.

## Takeaway

The main contribution is a prompt for practitioners to reflect on where AI can deliver practical value first. It highlights that AI readiness is organizational, department-specific, and tied to measurable business outcomes.
